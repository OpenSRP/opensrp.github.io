Supply Chain Management


The supply chain management features can help with minimizing stock outs and wastage by providing complete oversight of supplies available in the field. With a better understanding of usage and supplies available at the field level, purchasers at the national level will be able to effectively order and allocate supplies based on true demand.

Facilitated reporting has implications beyond decreased manual reportingp burdens. The digitization of data opens doors for analytics and real-time feedback at all management levels. Beyond this, reports on provider performance and supply availability are presented in easy to understand charts - enabling data-driven supervision, performance feedback, and quality control.

List of features:

  • Automated calculation of wastage
  • Show projections for vaccine use for next month
  • Register stock received, used, and wasted
  • Adjust stock balance
