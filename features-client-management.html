Client Management


Client management features promote continuity of care across life stages by ensuring clients receive the complete package of health services with automated scheduling and showing reminders for follow-up visits and required services. These features enable health workers to see a holistic view of their clients and easily manage client records.

List of features:

  • Automated online and offline health service scheduling and reminders for health workers
  • In-form calculations (e.g. high risk pregnancy calculation, expected delivery date, age from birthdate, nutritional status, z-score, etc.)
  • Data entry constraints
  • Clinical decision support algorithms
  • Automated growth chart plotting
  • Send SMS health service reminders
  • Send SMS health promotion messages
  • Dynamic search by specified parameters (e.g. ID number, name, QR code, facial recognition, etc.)
  • Dynamic filter by specified parameters (e.g. location, service prioritization, etc.)
  • Sort by specified parameters (e.g. alphabetical order, expected delivery date, service prioritization, etc.)
  • Record all visits and services provided
  • Display service history by client (including vaccination history, health indicators, vitals, etc.)
  • Edit/Update visit and service history
  • (In progress) User configurable views
  • (In progress) Map view of patient register
