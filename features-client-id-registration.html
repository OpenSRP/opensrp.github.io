About OpenSRP


Client identification & registration features provide governments with a reliable tool for collecting data at the source, resulting in a consistent denominator for calculating health indicators. Client enumeration is key in determining realistic and effective health coverage targets. For example, vaccination coverage targets cannot realistically be set if it is unclear how many people are actually in the population.

List of features:

  • Auto-generated unique IDs
  • Remove client from registry (in case of death or lost to follow-up)
  • Register clients
  • Register client demographic information (e.g. household, head of household, siblings, etc.)
  • Register client out of catchment area
  • Offline data entry
  • Record client's photo
  • Edit/Update demographic, contact/personal client information
